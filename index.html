03

Semantic Defect Search

Hybrid search over a decade of defect reports at GE Healthcare, answering one question: have we seen this before? Dense, BM25 and ColBERT retrieval fused with Reciprocal Rank Fusion and a cross-encoder reranker, with log-line normalization that pushed retrieval accuracy into the mid-90s. Every design choice was measured against a recall@k evaluation harness. Runs on-prem in Docker and is queried from a shared CLI or straight from AI coding assistants over MCP.

  • Hybrid retrieval
  • Reranking
  • Docker
  • MCP
